Questions from Clockwise Users

Can I migrate my Clockwise data to CalWizz?

You don't need to. CalWizz reads directly from your Google Calendar via OAuth — the same events Clockwise used are already there. Just connect your calendar and you're set.

Does CalWizz auto-reschedule flexible meetings like Clockwise?

Not yet — this is our #1 priority and is actively in development. Today, CalWizz protects your focus time by creating holds. Automatic meeting rescheduling is coming soon.

Is CalWizz really free?

Yes. The free tier includes schedule health scoring, focus time blocking, lunch protection, meeting analytics, and the Chrome extension. No credit card, no time limit. Pro adds advanced analytics and export for $5/month.

How does the Chrome extension work?

Install from the Chrome Web Store, sign in with your CalWizz account, and you'll see your health score on the toolbar badge and as a floating widget on calendar.google.com. It updates every 15 minutes.

What about Outlook / Microsoft 365 support?

Currently Google Calendar only. Outlook support is on our roadmap for later this year.

Is my calendar data safe?

CalWizz uses Google OAuth with encrypted token storage. We never store your event content permanently — analysis happens in real-time. Read our privacy policy for details.
